## Steps

1. Open the PDF in Preview by double-clicking it in Finder
2. Go to `File > Export as PDF...`
3. Remove the second .pdf and save the file
4. There will be a dialog that asks to replace it, click `Replace`

That's it. The password protection has been removed from the PDF.
